## Notes

- Rates are per-piece USD. Not per-kg — piece-based pricing.
- Above threshold → ML subsidizes shipping more (lower cost to seller).
- Below threshold → seller pays more of the shipping cost.
- Above ~1.5kg the two tiers usually merge (same price regardless of threshold).
- Maximum listed weight is 15kg. For heavier items, extrapolate or use last entry.
